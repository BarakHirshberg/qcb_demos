Particle in a box  (infinite square well)

A particle confined to a box with infinite walls (V = 0 inside, ∞ outside). Switch between one particle in 1D, one particle in 2D, and two non-interacting particles in 1D. The last two are mathematically the same product wavefunction (x,y ↔ x₁,x₂). Lengths are in units of the box length L (= 1), energies in units of h²/8mL².
